About GamerTech Solutions LLC

Company Overview

Founded in 2018 and headquartered in Louisville, Kentucky, GamerTech Solutions LLC has established itself as a premier provider of VR procurement and export solutions. We serve enterprise clients ranging from Fortune 500 companies to innovative startups across manufacturing, healthcare, education, and real estate sectors.

To democratize access to cutting-edge VR technology through strategic procurement, seamless global distribution, and comprehensive technical support. We bridge the gap between innovative VR manufacturers and enterprises seeking to implement transformative virtual reality solutions.
